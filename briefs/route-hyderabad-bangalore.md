## 8. Overnight pattern (this route's signature)

This is one of the most-popular overnight booking corridors:

- **9-10 PM Hyderabad departure** → 5-6 AM Bangalore arrival.
- Used for: Monday-morning corporate starts, Friday-evening weekend escapes (Bangalore → Coorg/Mysore onward), red-eye corporate transfers to avoid daytime traffic.
- Night charges (₹250-500 depending on vehicle) apply for trips between 10 PM-6 AM.
- NH44 is well-lit and 24/7 commercial, but driver alertness matters — we assign senior drivers with NH44 night experience.

## 9. Monsoon advisory

- **Southwest monsoon (June-September):** dominant for both Hyderabad and Bangalore. Telangana side has moderate rains; the Anantapur belt is in a rain shadow and stays drier. Route operates without closures.
- **Northeast monsoon (October-December):** Bangalore side gets evening showers; Anantapur and Hyderabad less affected.
- **March-May (peak summer):** hot in Anantapur belt (38-42°C); cabin AC essential. Bangalore stays cooler (28-32°C).
- **December-February:** ideal — cool, dry, smooth.
- **Festival surges:** Sankranti (mid-January), Bonalu (Hyderabad festival, July-August), Karnataka Rajyotsava (November 1). Long-weekend Friday departures see ~20-30% surge in Hyderabad → Bangalore booking volume.
