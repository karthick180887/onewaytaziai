## 9. Monsoon advisory

- **Southwest monsoon (June-September):** Coimbatore region gets light-moderate rain; route operational. Salem-Erode stretch through hills can have brief shower episodes.
- **Northeast monsoon (October-December):** primary rain for Chennai side; flooding rare on this corridor since it heads west away from coast.
- **March-May:** hot inland (35-40°C); AC essential. Coimbatore stays slightly cooler than Salem due to elevation.
- **Festival surge:** Pongal (Jan), Onam in Kerala (Sep) creates Kerala-direction crossover traffic.

## 10. Multi-day package framing

- **2-day Coimbatore + Ooty:** Day 1 Chennai → Coimbatore arrival. Day 2 Coimbatore → Ooty via Mettupalayam, optional return same day or 3-day with overnight Ooty.
- **3-day Coimbatore + Munnar:** Day 1 Chennai → Coimbatore. Day 2 Coimbatore → Munnar via Pollachi. Day 3 Munnar → return Chennai (long, 13 hrs — or split with Madurai overnight).
